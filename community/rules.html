Our Rules

Even if we're not very professional, we still have community lobby rules. Please read these before heading to the channels.

Be respectful

Be respectful of your fellow man. You can crack jokes (even at others' expense, if it's in good fun), but you aren't allowed to be a dick in general.

Be helpful

If you can, please try to help out whenever possible. If you can't, that's fine too.

Be cool

If a moderator, developer, or other important person contacts you about your behavior, please attempt to follow their guidelines. If they take action which you do not agree with, tell someone else on the crew!
